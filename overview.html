Forest Green Estates is a premier residential development nestled in Kiwatule – Naalya, one of Kampala's most sought-after and rapidly growing suburbs.

Comprising nine elegant blocks with a total of 144 fully furnished, 3-bedroom condominium units, the estate has been designed to offer an unparalleled living experience that seamlessly blends modern architecture and natural surroundings.

Each 156 m² unit features a fully fitted kitchen, luxury finishes, aluminium windows, solid timber doors, and a private terrace — all within a secure, resort-style estate.
